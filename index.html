About

Creativity. Expression. Freedom. Life The first requirement of a student is to learn. It is what defines a student. To learn- not to know- for there exists a significant difference between these two things. My life is an expression of my learning- my experiences- my growth and progress through life. A person is not measured by what they know- for the amount of this is constantly increasing and falling. But how we learn- why we learn- that is constant. Web development, speaking with words and without them, honesty, touching and feeling lives- these are the things that define my life. Meeting people- listening, speaking. Learning-This is my happiness.
I am a creative. I am a developer. I am a student. I am Tanaka.
